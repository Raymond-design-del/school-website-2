Our History

FR. Scheffer Boys School was established with the aim of providing quality education and nurturing discipline among young learners. Over the years, the school has grown into a respected institution known for academic excellence and strong values.

Our Mission

To provide quality education by instructing sense of discipline,hardwork and commitment to duty at Fr. Scheffer Community.

Our Vission

To mould and provide successful boys.

Our core Values

  1. Responsibility
  2. Spirituality
  3. Integrity
  4. Service
