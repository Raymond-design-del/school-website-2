Our History

FR. Scheffer Boys School was established with the aim of providing quality education and nurturing discipline among young learners. Over the years, the school has grown into a respected institution known for academic excellence and strong values.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive and disciplined learning environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential.

Vission

To be a leading institution recognized for excellence in education, character development, and innovation.

Our core Values
